Frequently asked questions

Is this QR code generator really free?

Yes. No account, no watermark, no usage limit. You can generate and download as many QR codes as you want.

Does the QR code expire?

No. The QR code itself never expires — a URL code will keep working for as long as the destination URL stays live; a text, phone, Wi-Fi, or contact code has the information built in and works forever.

What types of QR codes can I create?

A website link, plain text, a phone number, an email address, an SMS message, a Wi-Fi network login, or a contact card (vCard) that saves straight into someone's phone contacts when scanned.
